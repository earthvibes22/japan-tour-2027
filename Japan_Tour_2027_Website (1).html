The Story
26 Players. One Squad. A Whole Community Behind Us.

The countdown has officially begun. We're part of the St Peter's 1st XV rugby team, and we've been given an amazing opportunity to tour Japan in 2027 — elite international competition and a massive cultural experience all in one.

But getting our full squad of 26 players to the other side of the world takes some serious teamwork off the field too. That's where this page comes in: two ways to back us, built around what actually makes this team tick — the jersey we wear, and the people who back it.

Whether you sponsor a name on the jersey or come spend a day with us on the golf course, every bit of support gets us one step closer to making this trip happen.
